Haunting Henry

A Nightshade Glade Story  ·  Narrative Demo
Content Warnings
  • Domestic tension and emotional abuse
  • A missing child
  • Parental figures behaving erratically
  • Occult imagery (Wiccan protective items)
  • Mild body horror imagery in some endings
  • Nightmare sequences
  • Child in danger

This game does not contain graphic violence or sexual content. Some endings are significantly more unsettling than others — you'll know them when you find them.

It's a long weekend, Mom is out of town, and it's just you and Henry — your stepdad, certified dweeb, and the guy who always lets you win at video games. Except something is wrong with Henry this weekend. The door to his office keeps closing. He's up at midnight, muttering to himself. And there's smoke creeping under your bedroom door.

You could stay in bed. That would be the smart thing to do. But you've always been the kind of kid who needed to know.

Haunting Henry is a branching narrative game across three nights, where the items you find — and the choices you make — shape what you understand, what you keep, and what you lose. There are 64 possible endings. Some are quiet. Some are not.
