BareCat Studios invites players to visit Nightshade Glade — a town filled with friendly smiles, sprawling forests, and centuries of dark secrets. We make games for the kids who read scary stories by flashlight under their covers, and who always knew something was wrong with the adults.

Learn More
The Town

Nightshade Glade is an interconnected universe of campfire horror games — each one a ghost story that stands alone, but leaves embers that carry forward.

The Studio

BareCat Studios is a solo indie studio building pixel art horror games rooted in folklore, atmosphere, and the specific dread of knowing something the adults won't say.

The Promise

Every game we release is an invitation to care about what happens next. The universe deepens with every story we tell — and it all leads somewhere.

What we believe

Games are one of the most direct forms of storytelling ever created — an invitation to walk a mile in someone else's shoes. If someone is spending their limited and priceless time inside something we built, it had better be fun. And it had better mean something.
